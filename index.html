History of the Los Angeles Dodgers

Follow the Dodgers from their roots in Brooklyn to their modern legacy in Los Angeles — integrating baseball history, culture, and iconic moments.

The goal of this website is to educate and engage visitors by presenting a clear, accessible history of the Dodgers, organized by eras, championships, and key figures.

What You Will Find

  • Chronological overview from the Brooklyn Dodgers to the Los Angeles era.
  • Context for major moves, like the relocation to LA and the building of Dodger Stadium.
  • Spotlights on legendary players who shaped the franchise and the game.
  • Interactive features like “This Day in Dodgers History”.
