Team Origins — Brooklyn Dodgers

Early Years & Formation

The franchise we now know as the Dodgers began in Brooklyn in the late 19th century. Over the years, the team adopted several nicknames before “Dodgers” became common, reflecting Brooklyn pedestrians who dodged streetcars.

Brooklyn Championships

Brooklyn reached the World Series multiple times before finally winning the championship in 1955. That long-awaited victory over the New York Yankees remains one of the most celebrated moments in team history.

Jackie Robinson Era

In 1947, Jackie Robinson debuted with the Brooklyn Dodgers, breaking Major League Baseball’s color barrier. His courage and performance transformed the sport and American society.

Fast Facts

  • First Brooklyn World Series title: 1955.
  • Home ballpark: Ebbets Field.
