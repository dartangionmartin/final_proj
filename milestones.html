Hall of Fame Players & Awards

Across Brooklyn and Los Angeles eras, the Dodgers have produced numerous Hall of Fame players and MVPs. This includes Jackie Robinson, Sandy Koufax, Don Drysdale, and others whose contributions extend beyond statistics.

Did you know?

The 1988 World Series is remembered for Kirk Gibson’s dramatic pinch-hit home run in Game 1.
