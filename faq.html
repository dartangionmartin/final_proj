Frequently Asked Questions

Where do the stats and dates come from?

Statistics and dates are based on public baseball references such as MLB.com and Baseball-Reference.com. This site summarizes and links out to those resources for research.

Is this an official Dodgers or MLB site?

No. This is an educational project website created for a web development course. It is not affiliated with the Los Angeles Dodgers organization or Major League Baseball.

How should I use this site?

You can browse the eras, players, and milestones in any order. For a quick overview, start with the Home page and then explore the Brooklyn, Move to LA, and Milestones pages in that sequence.
