About This Website

Our Vision

To reduce confusion about baseball history by presenting the Dodgers story in a clear, organized format that anyone can explore.

Our Mission

To educate visitors with concise timelines, accessible explanations, and curated references so they can understand how the Dodgers evolved from Brooklyn to Los Angeles.

Our Values

  • We value accurate and unbiased historical information.
  • We value accessible design and clear navigation.
  • We value acknowledging original sources for statistics, images, and quotes.

Goal of the Project

The goal is to serve as a mini digital museum for Dodgers history, appropriate for fans, students, and teachers conducting research on the team and its cultural significance.
